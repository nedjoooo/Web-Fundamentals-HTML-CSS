Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> — <h6>

    <h1> Heading 1 </h1>
    <h2> Sub Heading 2 </h2>
    <h3> Sub Heading 3 </h3>

  • Paragraph Tags: <p>

    <p> This is my first paragraph </p>
    <h2> This is my second paragraph </h2>

  • Sections: <div> and <span>

    <div style="background: skyblue;">
           This is a div
    </div>
